Our Services

We tailor cleaning plans for daycares to keep children safe and facilities spotless. Below are our primary service offerings.

Daily & Weekly Daycare Cleaning

Routine cleaning focused on play areas, naptime spaces, food prep, and high-traffic zones. Flexible scheduling before or after hours to keep disruption minimal.

Washroom & Diaper Changing Disinfection

Specialized protocols for diaper changing stations and washrooms using non-irritating, baby-safe disinfectants.

Floors & Carpets

Daily floor care and scheduled carpet extraction to remove buildup and allergens.

Garbage Disposal & Waste Management

Sanitary removal of waste and sanitization of bins to prevent odours and cross-contamination.

High-touch Surface Disinfection

Regular disinfection of door handles, light switches, railings, toys (on rotation), and shared equipment.
