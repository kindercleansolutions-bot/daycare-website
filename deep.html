Deep Cleaning Program

Our monthly (or seasonal) deep cleaning restores facility freshness and removes hidden contaminants. Recommended for high-traffic centres and pre-inspection prep.

  • Indoor Windows: Interior glass and sills cleaned and polished.
  • Vents & HVAC openings: Dust removal and sanitization to improve air quality.
  • Wall cleaning: Spot and full wall washing to remove marks and build-up.
  • Deep Carpet Cleaning: Hot-water extraction and stain treatment.
  • Kitchen & Food Areas: Intensive degreasing and sanitizing on request.

Deep cleaning can be scheduled monthly or as-needed. We provide a checklist and before/after documentation for facility records.
