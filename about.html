About KinderClean

Founded to protect the littlest members of our communities, KinderClean Solutions provides professional, reliable and eco-conscious cleaning services for daycares across Canada.

Our team is trained in childcare-friendly cleaning protocols, background-checked, insured, and dedicated to transparent ingredient choices. We source Canadian-made, plant-based products wherever possible to minimize environmental impact and chemical exposure for infants and toddlers.
